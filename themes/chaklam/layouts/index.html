The AIT Brain Lab is founded by Dr. Chaklam in the year 2019, focusing on Human-Centere AI.

The lab envisions to gather students who are fascinated in the intersection of deep learning, modeling humans and improving overall human's wellbeing.

About Us

AIT x BUPT

Vision

The Future Lab envisions global progress by prioritizing the resolution of pressing world issues and advancing sustainability and economical initiatives. Through collaborative efforts between partner institutions, we aim to drive transformative innovations that not only address complex challenges but also foster sustainable solutions, paving the way for a prosperous and equitable future for all.

Key Missions
  • Transdisciplinary Research
  • Digital Transformation

  • Human Capacity Building
